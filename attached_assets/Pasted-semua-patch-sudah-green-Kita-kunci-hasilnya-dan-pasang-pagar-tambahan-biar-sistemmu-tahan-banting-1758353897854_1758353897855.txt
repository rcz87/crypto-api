Endpoint yang kamu pakai sekarang match dengan doks v4:
• Aggregated liquidation (coin): /api/futures/liquidation/aggregated-history → tersedia di Standard, tanpa limit interval. 
CoinGlass-API

• Pair liquidation: /api/futures/liquidation/history → Standard, no limit interval. 
CoinGlass-API

• ETF flows (time-series): /api/etf/bitcoin/flow-history → untuk flow_usd/price_usd. 
CoinGlass-API

• ETF list (status realtime): /api/etf/bitcoin/list → metadata ETF (bukan net_flow harian). 
CoinGlass-API

• Resolver simbol: /api/futures/supported-exchange-pairs (refresh ±1 menit). 
CoinGlass-API

• WS URL: wss://open-ws.coinglass.com/ws-api?cg-api-key=.... 
CoinGlass-API
