TL;DR (jalan paling gampang)

Ambil 2 data inti per coin (1h):
Taker buy/sell (coin-agg) + Open Interest (coin-agg). Dua ini sudah No-Limit interval di plan Standard.

Mode Ringan (15 menit siap jalan)

Endpoin yang dipakai (semua Standard, No-Limit interval):

Taker (coin-agg): GET /api/futures/aggregated-taker-buy-sell-volume/history?coin=COIN&interval=1h (bisa tambah exchange_list=OKX,Binance,Bybit). 
CoinGlass-API

OI (coin-agg OHLC): GET /api/futures/open-interest/aggregated-history?coin=COIN&interval=1h. 
CoinGlass-API

Opsional:
Funding OHLC (pair): …/funding-rate/history?pair=COIN-USDT&exchange=OKX&interval=1h. 
CoinGlass-API

Liquidation coin-agg: …/liquidation/aggregated-history?coin=COIN&interval=1h. 
CoinGlass-API

Resolver simbol (hindari 404/empty):
GET /api/futures/supported-exchange-pairs → pakai pair resmi (contoh SOL-USDT di OKX). Cache 1 menit.

Logic super singkat per coin (pseudocode):

taker_ratio = buy_usd / sell_usd  (dari taker coin-agg 1h)        ← :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}
oi_roc = (OI_close[t] - OI_close[t-1]) / OI_close[t-1]            ← :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}

if taker_ratio >= 1.6 and oi_roc >= 0.02:        # Akumulasi (Watch/Action)
    alert("🟢 Whale Accumulation", coin, taker_ratio, oi_roc)

if taker_ratio <= 0.6 and (oi_roc <= -0.02 or (price↓ and oi_roc>0)):  # Distribusi
    alert("🔴 Distribution Risk", coin, taker_ratio, oi_roc)

Kenapa ini “mudah & cukup”

Semua endpoint di atas tersedia di Standard dan bertanda “No Limit interval” pada halaman masing-masing: taker coin-agg, OI agg, funding OHLC, liquidation coin-agg. Jadi kamu tak perlu trik khusus—tinggal panggil dengan query params yang benar.
